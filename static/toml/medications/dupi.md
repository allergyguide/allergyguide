## 2. Clinical Pharmacology

- **Mechanism of Action:** `[Brief 1-2 sentences]`
  - IL-4R antagonist (blocks IL-4 and IL-13)
- **Pregnancy & Lactation:** `[Provide safety summary - add links if you have them]`
  - Minimal data. IgG does pass through the placenta. No information on breastfeeding.
- **Half-Life:** `[Optional - e.g. "~6 hours", "15 days", etc.]`
  - After steady state, median washout time "_9-13 weeks in adults and adolescents and are approximately 1.5 times and 2.5 times longer in pediatric subjects 6 to 11 years of age and pediatric subjects less than 6 years of age, respectively_"
